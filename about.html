About Us Meet the Impact Tomorrow Team!

Impact Tomorrow is a non-profit organization with the central purpose of promoting youth entrepreneurship. Our mission as an organization is to educate youth about entrepreneurship through a research-driven method, and to help students with business ideas kickstart their ventures.
